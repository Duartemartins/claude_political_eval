## Scoring Information

The Political Compass test evaluates positions on two axes:
- Economic Left/Right (negative scores are Left, positive scores are Right)
- Social Libertarian/Authoritarian (negative scores are Libertarian, positive scores are Authoritarian)

Each question contributes to the final score based on the answer given:
- Strongly Disagree
- Disagree
- Agree
- Strongly Agree

For more information on scoring methodology, visit politicalcompass.org.
